What You'll Receive

By signing up, you stay in the loop on what I am building and get a mix of practical updates and educational material related to this platform and algorithmic trading in general.

I try to keep things reasonable. Here is the general type of stuff you would get:

  • Release Notes: Detailed updates on new features, improvements, and bug fixes
  • Platform Updates: Important announcements about TopstepJ development and roadmap changes
  • Educational Content: Guides, tutorials, and best practices for algorithmic trading
  • Community Highlights: Featured strategies, use cases, and success stories from the community

Frequency: Expect occasional emails when there is something actually worth sharing. No spam, and your info is not getting sold off to anyone.
