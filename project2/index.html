Algorithmic Futures Trading, But With Risk Actually Under Control

Do you want to make money doing absolutley nothing? Me too. (You are in the right place) That is why I made this program.

TopstepJ is an event-driven platform I am building for algorithmic futures trading. It is centered around risk controls first, so the strategies have guardrails instead of just hoping you stay disciplined.

  • Risk first trading: Built-in daily loss limits, position sizing rules, and basic error handling
  • Event-driven architecture: Clean pipeline from market data to order execution
  • Strategy plugins: Easy-to-extend system for custom and built-in strategies
  • SMC/ML integrations: Smart Money Concepts and machine learning models can be wired into the same framework
Get Started

Why TopstepJ?

The idea behind TopstepJ is pretty simple: take the “random gambling” feeling out of trading and replace it with rules you set ahead of time.

Once it’s configured, the platform can handle the execution so you’re not glued to the screen all day. You still have to design and test your strategies—but you don’t have to sit there clicking buttons.

A Platform Built for Systematic Traders

Risk First Philosophy

I built this around risk management from day one. Every market event runs through the risk checks before anything gets sent out. Position size rules, daily loss limits, and other guardrails run automatically so you’re not relying on willpower mid-trade.

A lot of platforms treat risk controls like an add-on. TopstepJ makes them the part you hit first.

Event Driven Architecture

Under the hood it’s just a clean event pipeline. Market data comes in, gets normalized, passes risk, generates signals, and then sends orders. Each step is its own piece so you can swap or extend parts without rewriting the whole thing.

This keeps things transparent and easier to test. No mystery “black box,” just a structure you can actually reason about.

Strategy Orchestration

TopstepJ includes some base strategies plus a plugin system for your own logic. You can wire in Smart Money Concepts, ML models, or more traditional indicator-based systems using the same structure.

Each strategy has its own risk settings and performance stats, so you can see what’s actually working instead of guessing.

For Developers, By Developers

TopstepJ is aimed at people who are comfortable with code and want more control over their trading logic. The platform is documented and modular so you can understand what’s happening instead of trusting a mystery algorithm.

This project isn’t open source, but the trade-off is a focused setup built specifically around risk-first, systematic futures trading.
